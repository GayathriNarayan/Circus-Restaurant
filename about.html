About Us

Historic circus in Stockholm Central is the place to be for fun, socialising and pushing boundaries, with the ambition to create extraordinary, unusual and unexpected experiences. The restaurant inspired by the odd and unexpected that circus once was.

At the our restaurant, you feel part of a community and away from it all, bring your extended family or come by yourself. You can come if you want to escape from reality, to experience something extraordinary, or if you just want to be on your own for a while.

Our restaurant is based on a close collaboration with passionate farmers, growers, breeders, fishermen and hunters who share our values ​​both in terms of quality, but also the environment and ethics.

We have chosen to focus on our Swedish raw materials, the proximity contributes to a good collaboration with producers and it triggers our creativity. Our Swedish climate and the dramatic differences in seasons give us a great variety in supply over the year and no two days are the same.
